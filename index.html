GDSC Solution Challenge

The Kisumu National Polytechnic

About the Challenge

The GDSC Solution Challenge is an annual event that brings together students from around the world to develop innovative solutions to real-world problems. This year's challenge is focused on addressing the challenges faced by communities in the Kisumu region.

Challenges

  • Access to Education: Develop a solution to improve access to quality education for students in the Kisumu region.
  • Healthcare: Develop a solution to improve access to affordable and quality healthcare for residents of the Kisumu region.
  • Environmental Sustainability: Develop a solution to address the environmental challenges faced by the Kisumu region, such as pollution, waste management, and deforestation.
  • Economic Empowerment: Develop a solution to empower the people of the Kisumu region economically, such as by creating jobs, promoting entrepreneurship, and supporting small businesses.
  • Social Inclusion: Develop a solution to promote social inclusion and reduce discrimination against marginalized groups in the Kisumu region.

Submission Guidelines

  • Teams of up to 5 students are allowed.
  • Solutions must be submitted by April 15, 2023.
  • Solutions must be submitted in the form of a project proposal.
  • Project proposals must be submitted electronically to gdsc@kisumupoly.ac.ke.
  • Project proposals will be evaluated by a panel of judges.
  • The top 5 teams will be selected to present their solutions at the GDSC Solution Challenge Finals on May 1, 2023.

Prizes

  • The winning team will receive a cash prize of KES 100,000.
  • The second-place team will receive a cash prize of KES 50,000.
  • The third-place team will receive a cash prize of KES 25,000.
  • All finalists will receive certificates of participation.

Contact

For more information, please contact the GDSC Solution Challenge Committee at gdsc@kisumupoly.ac.ke.
